<p>The ECG Counsellor is usually available every&nbsp;<strong>Monday</strong> and <strong>Wednesday</strong>,
and sessions will be conducted at the&nbsp;<strong>Meeting Room</strong>&nbsp;(next
to the ECG Corner @ the Atrium).</p>
<p>To discuss any of the following, please book appointment slots that are
during your&nbsp;<strong>recess / lunch breaks / free periods</strong>&nbsp;or&nbsp;<strong>after your curriculum hours</strong>.</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Raising self-awareness (<strong>VIPS, RIASEC</strong>, etc.)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Subject combination (e.g. <strong>Sec 2 / JC</strong>)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Post-Secondary</strong>&nbsp;options / pathways</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Preparation for different <strong>Admission Exercises</strong>, e.g. JIE,
JAE, DSA-JC, EAE, etc.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Factors to consider in <strong>ECG planning</strong>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Any other <strong>ECG related</strong>&nbsp;questions</p>
</li>
</ul>

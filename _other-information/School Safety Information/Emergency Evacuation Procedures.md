<p><strong><u>Emergency Evacuation Drill</u></strong></p>
<p>All Junyuan Secondary School students should familiarise themselves with the emergency evacuation procedure so that they will be able to react in a calm and speedy manner should an emergency occur within the school premises. A copy of evacuation routes is displayed in all room</p>
<p><strong>Upon activation:</strong></p>
<ul type="disc">
<li>There will be a continuous ringing of the alarm (about 1 minute) followed by an announcement over the PA system to confirm evacuation.</li>
<li>Everyone is to remain in position and keep quiet. If you are outside, proceed to the nearest room/place with a PA speaker where you can hear the announcement.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Evacuation procedures during emergency drill</strong></p>
<img style="width: 80%;" src="/images/eep1.jpg" />
<p><strong>At Assembly Area:</strong></p>
<ul type="disc">
<li>Refrain from talking and making noise.</li>
<li>Listen to instructions.</li>
<li>Stay within your lines and with your class.</li>
<li>Do not leave the assembly area unless with permission from the teachers.</li>
<li>Stay calm and be patient.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Stand down/ Termination of Emergency</strong></p>
<ul type="disc">
<li>The P/VP will announce the stand down.</li>
<li>Further instructions/ debriefing will then be given for the next course of actions.</li>
</ul>

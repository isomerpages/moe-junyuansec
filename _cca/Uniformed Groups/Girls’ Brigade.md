<p>The Girls’ Brigade Singapore is a values-driven uniformed group that teaches Girls to lead and serve by example – to put others before self. In the Girls’ Brigade, ‘Servant Leadership’ changes the leader in every Girl as she changes the world.</p><p>The Objective of the Girls’ Brigade is to develop each Girl and Officer to her fullest potential by Equipping, Empowering and Enabling every Girl to be a leader, and every Officer a servant leader.</p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 80%;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/gb1.jpg"></div><h4><strong>Our Method</strong></h4><table><tbody><tr><td rowspan="1" colspan="1"><p>Uniform &amp; Identity</p></td><td rowspan="1" colspan="1"><p>Uniform is a great way to instil a sense of identify and belonging.</p></td></tr><tr><td rowspan="1" colspan="1"><p>Structure for Mentoring &amp; Leadership</p></td><td rowspan="1" colspan="1"><p>We believe in placing Girls in situations that enable them to exercise leadership skills, under the watch guidance of our Teachers and Officers.</p></td></tr><tr><td rowspan="1" colspan="1"><p>Dedicated Volunteers</p></td><td rowspan="1" colspan="1"><p>We have a team of dedicated Volunteer Adult Leaders (VALs) who are endorsed by MOE. Our VALs are trained to deliver the GB programmes.</p></td></tr><tr><td rowspan="1" colspan="1"><p>Rewarding Achievement</p></td><td rowspan="1" colspan="1"><p>We adopt a system of rewarding Girls with badges after the completion of each activity. This is intended to motivate our Girls to perform to the best of their abilities in everything they do.</p></td></tr></tbody></table><h4><strong>Motto</strong></h4><p>"Every Girl a Leader, Every
